BreezeBooks — Privacy Policy Last updated: July 2026 1. What This Tool Is BreezeBooks is a private, internal software tool used solely by Our Savior Lutheran Church ("the Organization") to synchronize donor and giving records between the Organization's own Breeze ChMS account and the Organization's own QuickBooks Online account. It is not a public product, and it is not used to collect data from, or on behalf of, anyone outside the Organization. 2. What Data Is Processed BreezeBooks processes donor information already stored in the Organization's Breeze ChMS account — including names, contact information (email, phone, address), and giving/contribution records — for the purpose of creating and updating matching records in the Organization's QuickBooks Online account. 3. Where Data Is Stored - Donor and giving data is read from Breeze and written to QuickBooks Online — both of which are the Organization's own accounts, governed by Breeze's and Intuit's own privacy policies. - BreezeBooks itself runs locally on computers operated by authorized members of the Organization. It does not upload or transmit data to any third-party server beyond Breeze's and QuickBooks' own APIs. - API credentials (keys and tokens) used to connect to Breeze and QuickBooks are stored locally, outside of any cloud-synced folder, and are never transmitted anywhere other than to Breeze's and Intuit's own official APIs. - Activity logs are kept locally for troubleshooting purposes; donor names, email addresses, and phone numbers are partially masked in these logs. 4. Who Has Access Access to BreezeBooks is restricted to individuals specifically authorized by the Organization, each with their own account and a role limiting what they can see and do within the tool. 5. Data Sharing BreezeBooks does not share, sell, or transmit any data to any party other than the Organization's own Breeze ChMS and QuickBooks Online accounts, as necessary to perform the synchronization described above. 6. Changes This policy may be updated at any time to reflect changes in how BreezeBooks is used internally by the Organization. Contact: For questions about this privacy policy, contact the Organization directly.
